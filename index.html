Questions

Before you begin

Do I need to be "religious" for this to help me?

The program is built on biblical principles (KJV) because order is a spiritual reality first. You'll get the most from it if faith matters to you — but the framework itself will bring clarity to anyone willing to look honestly.

How much time does it take?

The diagnostic takes about 5 minutes. Each week's module is short and practical — the real work is the daily disciplines, which you build into the time you already have.

What if I fall behind?

You can't fall behind — the 30-day cycle is designed to be repeated. If a week slips, you Review, Realign, and Renew. The rhythm always welcomes you back.

Is it self-paced?

Yes. Module 1 and the diagnostic open immediately; the rest unlock weekly so you're never overwhelmed, and everything stays yours to revisit.

What do I get the day I join?

Instant access to The Mirror diagnostic and Module 1 (IDENTIFY), plus your place in the Founding community.
